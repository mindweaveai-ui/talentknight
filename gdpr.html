Privacy & Data

Last updated: May 2025  ·  TalentKnight Ltd

1. Who We Are

TalentKnight ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered recruitment platform connecting employers with pre-vetted candidates. We are committed to protecting your personal data and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Data Controller: TalentKnight Ltd
Contact: privacy@talentknight.co.uk

2. What Data We Collect

For Candidates

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • CV / resume and work history
  • Skills, qualifications, and professional experience
  • LinkedIn profile (if provided)
  • Job preferences and availability
  • UTM source data (how you found us)

For Employers / Clients

  • Company name, contact name, email, phone number
  • Role briefs and hiring requirements
  • Account login credentials (via LinkedIn OAuth)
  • Billing information (processed securely via Stripe)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Match candidates to employer role briefs using our Vesper AI system
  • Communicate with you about relevant opportunities or services
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Improve the platform and our matching algorithms
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil services you've requested
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our platform and matching quality
  • Consent: Marketing communications (you may withdraw at any time)
  • Legal obligation: Where required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain candidate profiles for up to 24 months from the date of submission, or until you request deletion. Client account data is retained for the duration of the contract plus 6 years for legal compliance purposes.

6. Third Parties We Work With

  • Airtable — CRM and candidate data storage
  • Make (Integromat) — Workflow automation
  • Instantly — Email outreach
  • Clay — Data enrichment
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Vercel — Website hosting
  • LinkedIn — OAuth login

All third-party processors are GDPR-compliant and subject to data processing agreements.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request below or email privacy@talentknight.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies without your consent. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure third-party infrastructure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected individuals and the ICO within 72 hours where required.

10. Contact & Complaints

If you have questions about this policy or wish to raise a complaint, contact us at privacy@talentknight.co.uk.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk
