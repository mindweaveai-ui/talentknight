Legal & Privacy

Your data,
your rights.

TalentKnight is committed to handling personal data responsibly, transparently, and in compliance with UK GDPR. This page explains what we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

Last updated: April 2025  ·  TalentKnight Ltd  ·  Southend-on-Sea, Essex

Who we are

TalentKnight is an AI-powered recruitment matching platform operated by TalentKnight Ltd, based in Southend-on-Sea, Essex, United Kingdom. We act as a data controller for personal data collected through our website, demo platform, and client portal.

For any data-related enquiries, contact us at: privacy@talentknight.com

What we collect

We collect personal data in the following contexts:

  • Contact form submissions — name, company, email address, phone number, and any message you provide when enquiring via our website or demo.
  • Client portal usage — role briefs entered by clients, search history, and any CVs uploaded for AI scoring. CVs may contain personal data about third parties.
  • Website analytics — anonymised usage data to understand how our site is used. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (as defined under UK GDPR Article 9) unless explicitly provided to us.

Our lawful basis

We process personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Legitimate interests — responding to enquiries, providing our service to clients, and improving our platform.
  • Contract performance — processing data necessary to deliver the service to paying clients.
  • Consent — where you have explicitly opted in to marketing communications.

How we use your data

  • To respond to enquiries and provide information about TalentKnight
  • To onboard and service paying clients
  • To process role briefs and generate AI candidate shortlists
  • To score CVs uploaded by clients against role briefs
  • To send service-related communications (not marketing, unless you have opted in)
  • To maintain records for legal and contractual purposes

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

How long we keep it

We apply the following retention periods:

  • Contact enquiries — retained for 12 months from submission, then deleted.
  • Client account data — retained for the duration of the client relationship plus 6 months after contract end.
  • Uploaded CVs — deleted within 30 days of upload unless the client has actively saved the record.
  • Search history — retained for 6 months, then automatically purged.

You may request earlier deletion at any time — see your rights below.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

Right of access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request).

Right to erasure

Request that we delete your personal data. We will action this within 30 days.

Right to rectification

Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data we hold about you.

Right to portability

Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (CSV or JSON).

Right to object

Object to processing of your data where we rely on legitimate interests.

Right to restrict

Request that we limit how we use your data while a complaint is resolved.

To exercise any of these rights, use the form below or email us at privacy@talentknight.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by UK GDPR.

Cookies & tracking

TalentKnight uses only essential cookies required to operate the service:

  • Session storage — used to maintain your login state during a session. Cleared when you close your browser.
  • Local storage — used to store your search history and preferences within the client portal. Stored only on your device.

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or third-party analytics that identify individual users.

Third parties & processors

We use the following third-party services to operate TalentKnight:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — processes role briefs and CV content to generate candidate matches. Content is not used to train AI models.
  • EmailJS — used to send email notifications when a lead submits a contact form.
  • Vercel — our hosting provider. Infrastructure based in the EU/UK.
