Capabilities

Built for the way
hiring actually works.

Every feature designed for speed, quality, and control — not for the recruiter's process, but for yours.

Live AI matching

Every brief generates a fresh, contextual shortlist. The AI reads nuance — seniority, sector, location, urgency — not just keywords. Each search is different because each role is different.

Fit scores that mean something

Every candidate is ranked 0–100 against your specific brief. Not generic ratings — contextual scores with a written explanation of why each person fits. Know instantly where to focus your time.

FTSE-quality candidate library

150+ pre-qualified candidates spanning 30 role types — CFO to site manager, data scientist to ward nurse. Backgrounds include HSBC, AstraZeneca, Tesco, Vodafone, BP, Unilever, and more.

Lead capture and follow-up

Interested clients leave their details in seconds. You see every lead in your admin dashboard with a one-click email link — no spreadsheets, no missed enquiries, no chasing.
